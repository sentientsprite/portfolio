## The Problem

A B2B client was manually managing lead follow-ups, resulting in slow response times and inconsistent nurture sequences. Leads were falling through the cracks between marketing and sales.

## The Solution

I built an automated email funnel using Zapier and HubSpot:

1. **Lead scoring model** — Assigned points based on page visits, form submissions, and email engagement
2. **Automated triggers** — Zapier workflows routed high-score leads to sales and nurtured lower-score leads
3. **Segmented sequences** — Different email paths based on industry, company size, and engagement level

## The Impact

- **80% of nurture emails** fully automated
- **35% boost** in email engagement rates
- **40% faster** lead response times
- **2,000+ contacts** added to automated nurture sequences

## Tools Used

Zapier for workflow automation, HubSpot for CRM and email delivery, and Google Analytics for tracking attribution across the funnel.
