## The Challenge

A wellness brand was seeing strong traffic but weak conversion rates on their product landing pages. Bounce rates were high, and the existing pages weren't optimized for mobile or search intent.

## The Approach

I redesigned multiple themed Shopify landing pages with a focus on three areas:

1. **Copy optimization** — Rewrote headlines and CTAs based on customer pain points and search intent
2. **Visual hierarchy** — Restructured layouts to guide users toward conversion actions
3. **Technical SEO** — Implemented schema markup, optimized meta tags, and improved page speed

## Results

- **45% increase** in conversion rate across tested pages
- **28% reduction** in bounce rate
- **30+ point improvement** in Google PageSpeed scores

## Key Takeaways

Landing page performance isn't just about design — it's about aligning copy, visuals, and technical SEO with user intent. A/B testing every major change ensures data-driven decisions rather than assumptions.
