## Background

RidgeCrest Herbals needed high-performing landing pages for seasonal product launches. Existing pages had generic templates that didn't reflect brand identity or optimize for conversion.

## Strategy

### Phase 1: Research
- Analyzed competitor landing pages in the herbal wellness space
- Reviewed heatmaps and session recordings from existing pages
- Identified top-performing copy patterns from past campaigns

### Phase 2: Design & Build
- Created themed Shopify landing page templates for each product line
- Implemented mobile-first responsive design
- Added trust signals: reviews, certifications, and guarantees

### Phase 3: Testing & Optimization
- Ran A/B tests on headlines, hero images, and CTA placement
- Iterated based on conversion data over 6-week cycles
- Optimized page speed to sub-3-second load times

## Results

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Conversion Rate | 2.1% | 3.0% | +45% |
| Bounce Rate | 62% | 45% | -28% |
| PageSpeed Score | 58 | 91 | +33 pts |
| Avg. Session Duration | 1:12 | 2:45 | +129% |

## Lessons Learned

The biggest wins came from aligning landing page messaging with the specific search intent behind each ad campaign, rather than using one-size-fits-all templates.
