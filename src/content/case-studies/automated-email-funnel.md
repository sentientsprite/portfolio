## Client Context

A B2B technology company was struggling with lead management. Marketing generated leads through Google Ads and content, but the handoff to sales was manual and slow.

## Implementation

### Lead Scoring Framework
Built a point-based scoring system in HubSpot:
- **Page visits**: +5 points per high-intent page
- **Form submissions**: +20 points
- **Email opens**: +3 points
- **Email clicks**: +10 points
- **Demo requests**: +50 points

### Automation Workflows
Using Zapier, I connected:
1. **Form submissions** → HubSpot contact creation → Lead score assignment
2. **Score thresholds** → Route to sales (50+) or nurture sequence (<50)
3. **Abandoned cart** → SMS recovery via Klaviyo integration
4. **Weekly reports** → Automated dashboard to stakeholders

### Email Sequences
Designed three nurture paths:
- **Awareness**: Educational content, case studies, industry reports
- **Consideration**: Product comparisons, demo invitations, testimonials
- **Decision**: Pricing guides, limited-time offers, direct sales outreach

## Results

- **80%** of nurture emails fully automated
- **35%** increase in email engagement
- **40%** reduction in lead response time
- **12%** abandoned cart recovery rate via SMS
- **2,000+** new contacts in automated sequences

## Tech Stack

HubSpot CRM, Zapier, Klaviyo, Google Analytics, Python (custom reporting scripts)
